FocusGuard Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

🔒 FocusGuard is a fully offline app. It collects no personal data, transmits nothing over the internet, and requires no account. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

FocusGuard is an Android application developed by [Your Name / Solo Developer]. The app helps users voluntarily limit their time on social media apps through self-set daily limits and cognitive puzzle challenges.

Contact: [your-email@example.com]

2. Data We Collect

FocusGuard stores the following data locally on your device only, in an SQLite database that is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your self-configured daily app time limits
  • App usage session timestamps (which apps you blocked, when)
  • Puzzle attempt records (correct/incorrect, difficulty level)
  • In-app achievement and streak records
  • App settings and onboarding preferences

We do not collect: your name, email, phone number, location, device ID, advertising ID, or any personally identifiable information.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics or crash reporting data
  • No advertising data or third-party tracking
  • No biometric or health data
  • No payment information
  • No social media account data
  • No cloud sync or backup data

The app has no INTERNET permission in its Android manifest. It is architecturally incapable of transmitting data.

4. Android Permissions Used

FocusGuard requests the following Android system permissions. Each is used solely for app-blocking functionality:

Permission Why it's needed
PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS Detects when a blocked app (e.g. Instagram) is in the foreground so the time limit can be enforced
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Draws the block screen overlay over the blocked app when your daily limit is exceeded
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Keeps the background monitor running reliably with a persistent notification
BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE Used for faster app-launch detection only — no actions are performed on your behalf
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Sends local notifications (e.g. "5 minutes left today") — no server involved

5. Data Sharing

We share your data with no one. There are no third-party SDKs, no analytics platforms, no advertising networks, and no cloud services integrated into FocusGuard.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete all locally stored data at any time from within the app (Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete All Data). Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

7. Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child under 13 is using this app, please contact us and we will assist with data deletion.

8. Security

FocusGuard enforces the following security measures by design:

  • No internet permission — zero network egress possible
  • android:allowBackup="false" — data cannot be backed up to Google servers
  • android:usesCleartextTraffic="false" — no unencrypted communication
  • All database queries use parameterized bindings — no SQL injection risk
  • All services are declared exported=false

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
